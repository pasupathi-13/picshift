How to use

  • 1. Upload a photo containing a logo, icon, or trademark.
  • 2. Adjust the brush size and paint a red mask over the logo region.
  • 3. Click Erase Logo. The Laplace diffusion algorithm will automatically replace the logo with surrounding pixels.
  • 4. Download your cleaned image. Repeat on other areas if necessary.

100% Private & Client-Side

Your photos are processed entirely inside your browser cache. They are never sent to a server.
